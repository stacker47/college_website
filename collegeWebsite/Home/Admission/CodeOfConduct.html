Code of Conduct

Code of Conduct

  • Admission of candidates found guilty of using unfair means in examination will be cancelled.
  • Candidates found misbehaving with teachers and/or college staff will be penalized as per the college regulations and admission of such candidates will be cancelled.
  • Getting admission by suppressing facts will be treated as a breach of trust. Admission of such candidates will also be cancelled.
  • Admission of candidates taking part in agitations shall be cancelled as per college rules.
  • Rules regarding attendance/sessional work/tutorials/practical laboratory work shall be applicable to all students. Negligence of the above may result in disqualification.
  • Students are not allowed to bring their vehicles inside the college. They can keep their vehicles in the place provided for those.

N.B. For further details about admission rules & procedures, candidates are advised to read the prospectus carefully.

Rovers & Rangers

The D.A-V College Rovers and Rangers movement aims at contributing to the development of young people by helping them achieve their full physical, intellectual, social and spiritual significance as individuals. The movement strives to instil the sense of community feeling and mould the students as responsible citizens of the world.

Library

The Anand Swarup Central Library at D.A-V. College is a gateway to knowledge resources. Since its establishment in 1919 the library is striving to enhance the sphere of knowledge by inculcating analytical acumen in young minds. The library has three floors with numerous facilities. The library has a collection of over 95000 books and subscribes to over 30 periodicals. The collection of the library is growing by the day. With a view to improving library services, we are working towards the digitalization of the library.

Auditorium

The college has a huge auditorium with a seating capacity of about eight hundred. The auditorium is among the best in the region. It serves as the hub for all academic deliberations and cultural activities in the college. The auditorium is divided into various parts: the main floor, the wings, the rear event, the balcony, and the antechambers. The British architectural excellence accords a very special aura to the auditorium.

Research Labs

The college has a number of well-equipped laboratories, which serve as a home to a number of highly sophisticated experimental research facilities. These research laboratories are designed to serve staff scientists, students as well as research scholars. The interdisciplinary composition of these labs makes it possible to study structures from a number of combined and integrated perspectives in an attempt to provide research and consultations towards new theories. For more information about the facilities, please check with the respective departments.

Instrumentation Lab

The D.A-V. College Instrumentation Lab is one of the finest in the entire region. The college recognizes the power of modern instrumentation in dealing with the problems fundamental to the study of science and technology. The centre possesses a wide range of equipments for use by students and researchers in laboratory courses and research projects. Major instrumental equipment includes: IR Spectrometer, HPLC,P H Meter, U.V. Visible Spectrometer, Water Soil Analyzer, Auto Centrifuge Machine, Auto Titrator, P.C.R. Machine, B.O.D. Machine, Rotatory Flask Shaker,

Games and Sports

Sports & Games have a very important role to play in the growth and development of mind and body. The mission of College Games & Sports is to embrace the holistic development of our programs, with a focus on academic excellence, athletic success, spiritual mentoring and social growth. The college offers student participation in a bundle of sports activities including swimming, Cricket, Basketball, Hockey,Volleyball,Athletics,Weight Lifting,Handball,football. The college has a huge sprawling lush green playground which is one of the best in the city and the region. There is also an indoor stadium for indoor games like Badminton, Table Tennis.

Common Rooms

The college provides separate common rooms for boys and girls. The girls' common room is situated on the first floor above the principal's office. It has all the basic amenities like magazines, drinking water, toilet, and proper sitting facilities. The boys' common room is situated close to the Students' Union building. It has all necessary amenities to make the students feel comfortable.
